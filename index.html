How to use the app
Create a new note and move it to planned notes, by pressing the New button and Move to Planned button at the bottom.
Once you are ready to do the task click on the note in Planned and click Move to Current.
Once you have finished the task, you can move it to the Done list by clicking Move to Done.
To remove a note use the Delete/Clear button.
To edit a note just click on the note you want to edit and click on To Editor where you can edit your note. Then click the appropriate Move button once you are done editing.
